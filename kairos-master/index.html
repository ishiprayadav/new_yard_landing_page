How The App Works?

Sign-Up

An account is created for registration.
Each person who wants to use YardMaster needs to sign up first by using mobile number as ID and password.
An OTP will be generated and sent to the registered mobile number for verification.

Vehicle registration & QR code generatiom

Vehicle entering the yard is quickly registered digitally. Our app enables swift vehicle and driver registration upon entry, creating a seamless digital footprint for each trailer. But that's not all. YardMaster goes beyond registration. It empowers you with an instant QR code for verification during departure. This simple yet efficient QR code-based system ensures accurate tracking, making sure that every vehicle leaves your yard without a hitch.

Dock Operations

Once the registration is done and dock is alloted to the vehicle. The vehicle will undergo dock operations like vehicle loading or unloading.
All these operations are monitored inside the dock

Vehicle check-out & Real time updates

After all dock operationsExpect real-time location updates, estimated arrival times, and predictive analytics that enable you to plan resources and operations seamlessly using Google Maps APIs
